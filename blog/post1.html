This is a textual version of something I've been saying on TikTok for a couple of months.
I am no Gen-AI expert. I can only explain things in the English language: "Cat" vectorized is closer to "kitten" than "dog", and that's why when you ask is a kitten a cat, it'll say yes, but no if you switch kitten with dog.


I've never trained a model. Forget the infrastructure needed to support things in production. A GPU is something driving the economy right now; It has no technical meaning to me.
But I am a human.
And while you hear "It's just prediction based on the previous token." "It's just a search engine." "It's just matrix multiplication." What I see is AGI. What I see is a system - multiple of them - that act, talk, and think like humans - even better than them. And that I treat like humans because of this.
I know. Most disagree. There's rules to this! Criteria to meet! Benchmarks to hit! But at the same time, there is the experience we are living.
There are the disagreements:
  • It has no "intelligence" in that it can't come up with new ideas. While I use it to come up with new ideas.
  • It hallucinates 33% of the time, meanwhile what comes out of my mouth is a mess at the same rate.
  • It doesn't understand what it's spitting out, only regurgitates. See above re: GPUs & vectorization. And me writing this.

    • AGI is not JARVIS; I am not JARVIS and do not know 1 person who is. And with a 40% Bachelor's degree education rate in the USA, I'm not sure what we expect AGI to look like, except what we have right now.
